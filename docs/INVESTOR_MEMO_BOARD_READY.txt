====================================================================
EXECUTIVE THESIS
====================================================================
EventFlow is building a GDPR-first, AI-native event operations platform
for EU organizers. The strategy is to sit between low-end ticketing
tools and high-friction enterprise suites.

Core advantages:
- Event management hierarchy and execution quality (Cvent-like structure).
- Advanced registration flows (group-level forms, branching, invitations).
- Event-level drag-and-drop email builder (advanced columns, branding).
- AI agents with governance (approve-first for critical actions).
- Travel orchestration with Amadeus as priority integration.

====================================================================
4-WEEK MVP PLAN (15 MAR 2026 - 12 APR 2026)
====================================================================
WEEK 1 - PLATFORM FOUNDATION
- Multi-tenant organizations and strict data isolation.
- RBAC: Owner, Admin, Planner, Onsite, Finance, Viewer.
- Audit logging + GDPR baseline controls.
- Stripe seat-based billing foundation.

WEEK 2 - REGISTRATION EXCELLENCE
- Event management section finalized for consistency and simplicity.
- Drag-and-drop form builder per group.
- Complex branching and invitation logic.
- Sessions plugin activation path (capacity + waitlist).

WEEK 3 - COMMUNICATIONS + INTEGRATIONS
- Event-level email builder with advanced column layouts.
- Branding controls (logo/typography strategy).
- Email automations (GDPR-safe defaults, release 1 = email only).
- CRM integrations: Salesforce and HubSpot.
- Amadeus Phase A: flight/hotel search and pricing.

WEEK 4 - ONSITE + AI GOVERNANCE + HARDENING
- Badge printing + onsite check-in desk flow.
- AI in approve-first mode for sensitive operations.
- Security/performance hardening and beta rollout.

AMADEUS PRIORITY PLAN
====================================================================
Priority integration: Amadeus (first external platform integration).

Phase A (MVP):
- Flight Offers Search
- Flight Offers Price
- Hotel Search

Phase B (post-MVP):
- Flight Create Orders
- Hotel Booking

Why phased:
- Faster go-live within the 4-week window
- Lower support and operational risk
- Faster validation of travel demand inside event workflows

====================================================================
AI GOVERNANCE: APPROVE-FIRST (CLARIFICATION)
====================================================================
Approve-first means:
- Agents can prepare and suggest actions autonomously.
- Critical actions require explicit user confirmation before execution.
- Guarded actions include:
  - Bulk sends
  - Participant status changes
  - Refund and billing-impacting actions
  - Destructive or irreversible updates
